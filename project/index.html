What is AFROTC United

The purpose of this website is to inform users about the United States Reserved Officer Training Corps (AFROTC) program at UNC Charlotte. This site will tell you what it takes to become an officer in the United States Air Force. This site will allow you to explore and learn about the training and lifestyle of an AFROTC cadet.

This website will tell you everything you need to know about the AFROTC program at UNC Charlotte!

Intended Users

    Intended Users:

  1. The intended users of this website include anyone who is curious about the AFROTC program
  2. People wanting to learn how to become an officer in the United States Air Force (USAF)
  3. Individuals looking for a structured lifestyle
  4. Users wanting to join the Air Force
  5. Civilians wanting to learn more about AFROTC
